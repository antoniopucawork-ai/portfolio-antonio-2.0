EVOLUTION
MY EVOLUTION

Every project changes me.

This portfolio isn't a collection of finished work. It's a map of the moments that changed the way I think, design and build.

Jessica Website 1.0 Curiosity View Project →
Curiosity

Everything started with trust.

Jessica believed in me before I fully believed in myself. I didn't know how far this first project would take me. I only knew that I wanted to turn her idea into something real. It wasn't perfect, but it gave me the courage to keep building.

Growth

I began questioning every decision.

The second version wasn't just an attempt to make the website look better. I started asking why each element existed, what the user needed and how the experience could feel clearer and more intentional. I was no longer only learning how to build. I was beginning to understand how to design.

Jessica Website 2.0 Growth View Project →
Jessica Website 3.0 Identity View Project →
Identity

I started recognising myself in the work.

By the third version, I wasn't simply correcting what came before. I was making choices that felt increasingly mine: calmer spaces, stronger hierarchy, more thoughtful interactions and a clearer emotional direction. The website finally reflected Jessica's identity, but it also revealed the beginning of my own.

Growth became creation.
