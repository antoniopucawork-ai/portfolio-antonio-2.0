ADHD Daily

A personal digital product designed to transform mental chaos into clarity for people living with ADHD, overthinking and mental overload.

01

Why this project exists

ADHD Daily was born from the need for something simpler than another productivity app: a digital companion capable of understanding mental overload before asking the user to organise it.

02

The problem

Thoughts accumulate faster than they can be organised. Tasks, ideas, worries and reminders compete for attention, creating a constant sense of pressure and confusion.

03

The vision

The core experience begins with a Brain Dump: a space where the user can empty their mind without immediately having to decide, categorise or prioritise anything.
